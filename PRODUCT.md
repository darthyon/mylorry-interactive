# Product

## Register

product

## Users

**Host Portal (primary):** Internal ops staff at MyLorry HQ. High frequency, high familiarity. Configuring commission tiers, KPI targets, and SP account mappings across many agents. Power users who expect efficiency — they're in this screen repeatedly, not exploring it for the first time.

**Agent Portal:** Field sales agents reviewing their own commission dashboard. Lower technical literacy, infrequent visits. Checking KPI progress and payout history, not editing configuration.

**Context:** Desktop-first admin tool used at a desk, during business hours, under normal office light. Agents on mobile checking progress between site visits.

## Product Purpose

MyLorry.ai is a B2B fuel fleet management platform operating in the Malaysian market. The Host Portal manages the commercial layer: configuring how agents earn commission on fuel volume they drive, setting KPI targets, and managing SP (service provider) account assignments.

Success means: an ops staff member can review an agent's configuration, adjust KPI targets and thresholds for a new evaluation period, and save a new version — in under 2 minutes, without errors.

## Brand Personality

Bold · Confident · Modern

Tagline: MORE SAFETY · MORE SAVINGS · MORE EARNINGS

Voice: direct, no hand-holding, outcome-focused. The interface trusts users to know what they're doing. It states facts and lets the user act. It does not explain the obvious.

## Anti-references

- **Consumer fintech:** No gradient cards, no neon accent colors, no glowing revenue charts. This is not Robinhood or a crypto dashboard.
- **Legacy ERP:** No information crammed into every pixel. No dense form grids with 12 fields visible at once. No SAP-era table weight.

## Design Principles

1. **Confidence through density.** Internal power users don't need whitespace therapy. Pack information tightly but with clear hierarchy — never dense for density's sake.
2. **State tells the story.** Every piece of status data (KPI progress, commission tier, version) must communicate the right emotional tone for its actual state. A future period is neutral. A missed target is a warning. A clean record is a positive.
3. **Versioning is a feature, not a footnote.** The KPI configuration versioning system is meaningful — hosts need to trust that changes are auditable. Surface version context prominently, not as metadata.
4. **One edit path, total clarity.** The editing experience must sequence decisions logically: what period, what target, what thresholds, when does it take effect. No buried fields.
5. **Bold and precise, never decorative.** Visual emphasis should track information importance. Color and weight earn attention; they don't decorate.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

WCAG AA minimum. Color contrast 4.5:1 for body text. State changes (save, error, success) must not rely on color alone — use icons + text. Keyboard navigation for all primary edit flows.
